3D Designer Commission

Freelance Booth Design — Rates, Terms & Payment Structure
Kota Kinabalu, Sabah  ·  May 2025

Dear Designer,

Thank you for joining the Pro Event Solutions freelance design network. This document sets out the commission structure, project workflow, and payment terms applicable to all booth 3D design projects referred to you through our company. Please read this carefully before committing to any project.

Pro Event Solutions provides event supplies and full event management services across Kota Kinabalu and Tuaran, Sabah. As part of our growing offerings, we commission professional 3D booth design renderings for our clients. You are engaged as an independent designer under the terms below.

All projects begin with a free Phase 1 concept draft — one draft only, based on the client's budget and brief. This phase is unpaid. Your commission activates only once the client confirms and pays the 30% deposit, at which point you may proceed to Phase 2 detailed work.

Please note: Do not begin detailed Phase 2 work before the 30% deposit is confirmed received by Pro Event Solutions. Starting work early is entirely at your own risk and cannot be guaranteed for compensation.

Commission is calculated based on the project value and your applicable package rate. A minimum commission of RM 300 applies across all packages. In the event of project cancellation, you retain all milestone payments already disbursed — no refunds will be requested.

We look forward to a productive working relationship. If you have any questions regarding this agreement, please contact us before accepting a project.

Section 3
Key Terms & Conditions
01
Phase 1 is Unpaid
You provide one concept draft only, at no charge, based on the client brief and quotation provided by Pro Event Solutions. Do not invest more than this at the initial stage.
02
Minimum RM 300
Your commission will never fall below RM 300 per project regardless of the calculated amount. This protection applies to all packages without exception.
03
Cancellation Policy
If a project is cancelled at any stage, you retain all milestone payments already disbursed. No clawback. Payments made are final and non-refundable.
04
Deposit First
Never commence Phase 2 work until Pro Event Solutions confirms the 30% client deposit has been received. Early work is not covered under this commission agreement.
